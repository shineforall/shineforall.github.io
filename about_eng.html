SHINE

SHINE is a project to prepare for the next pandemic, such as COVID-19, funded by Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and KT, forming a consortium with four other organizations.

The aim of this study is to develop an AI algorithm to predict the risk of COVID-19 infection and the spread of infectious diseases based on Big Data collected through voluntary participation.

KT

Conducted following studies to reduce the spread of respiratory infectious diseases including COVID-19.

AI / Big Data-based Research Project
on Infectious Diseases

By analyzing the data entered through SHINE, we develop an algorithm that predicts the risk and spread of COVID-19.

SHINE APP

In response to emerging infectious diseases, an application called SHINE (Study of Health Information for Next Epidemic) has been developed.
