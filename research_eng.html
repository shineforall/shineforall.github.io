In collaboration with SHINE

We are conducting a research project with one of the world’s largest foundation, Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, to develop artificial intelligence-based infectious disease self-check algorithms and mobile self-check applications. In this project, research participants’ symptoms and PCR test results are collected in order to develop the next pandemic response technology. Furthermore, KT is working with hospitals and companies that specialize in each field to analyze telecommunication data and analyze the spread of infectious diseases.
All consortium members participating in SHINE project are striving to protect people’s health by minimizaing the spread of respiratory infectious diseases like COVID-19 and flu.
